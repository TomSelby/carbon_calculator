Welcome to the StranksLab travel emissions calculator, this is where we aim to calculate and record travel emissions from none day-to-day travel. Please use the links above to navigate between pages before calculating and submitting your estimations.

The graphs and charts below will be updated periodically as the database is populated, all data submitted is stored securley and only accessible by me (Tom, tas72@cam.ac.uk) however if you wish to remain annonmous please just leave the crsid section blank.

The method for calculating emissions is based off the UK government's conversion factors for the reporting of greenhouse emissions and the ICAO carbon emissions calculator for air travel. All code can be found on Github.
